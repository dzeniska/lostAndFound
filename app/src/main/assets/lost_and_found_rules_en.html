Rules for placing ads in the application:

 

1. General Provisions

 

1.1. In the Lost and Found application, you can place one type of ads-private.

 

1.2. a private advertisement submitted on behalf of an individual and not related to the implementation of entrepreneurial activity by this person. Ads submitted on behalf of individuals who do not have the appropriate state registration (registration with tax authorities), but are engaged in the systematic sale of goods (performance of works, provision of services), cannot be classified as private. Ads from real estate sales and rental agents also cannot be classified as private ads. If the facts of placing ads for the sale of the same type of goods from a private person are revealed, the Lost and Found application has the right to reject these ads.

 

2. The procedure for placing ads in the Lost and Found application

 

2.1. the period for placing ads

All ads in all categories are placed indefinitely.

 

2.2. deactivation of the ad

If the ad is no longer relevant, the user can deactivate it. To do this, select the "Delete" option on the ad viewing page.

 

2.3. duplicate

A user can submit only one ad for each specific thing.

 

3. General requirements for ads placed in the Lost and Found OFFICE application

 

3.1. Ads must be made in one of the official languages of the Republic of Belarus - Belarusian or Russian, and contain:

 

- the title of the ad, the description of a specific product. The description must be complete and reliable, not contain the user's contact details and links to other resources. Advertising of various goods is not allowed in one ad.

Mandatory contact information includes a phone number. The phone number is marked in a special field.

 

3.2. the ad must contain real photos of the subject. It is not allowed to post photos that contain a link to other resources and (or) their logos.

 

4. requirements for photos and conditions for their placement

 

4.1. the photo of the ad must reflect a certain copy being sold, in the form in which it was at the time of submitting the ad. So, for example, if you hit a mobile phone, then the ad should have a photo of your specific instance, and not just a photo of a phone of the same model and color as yours. It is not allowed to post photos taken from catalogs, from manufacturers' websites or other sites.

4.2. the same photo can only be used in one ad at a time.

4.3. the image should not contain links to other sites, as well as logos.

4.4. there should be no elements in the photo that can be regarded as offensive, illegal or calling for violence.

4.5. it is not allowed to post photos and other images of an erotic and pornographic nature, images with sexual overtones, including images of naked intimate parts of the body, people in transparent or open clothing, completely naked or people taking off their clothes.

4.6. it is prohibited to publish photos of slaughtered animals and their refreshing carcasses.

4.7. photographs containing the following types of ritual products are prohibited for publication: coffins, monuments with photos and data of the deceased, photographs of burial sites.

 

5. it is prohibited to place ads containing:

 

- propaganda or calling for violence, cruelty and the commission of illegal actions;

 

- information misleading consumers about plausibility;

 

- profanity, as well as offensive statements, including racist and religious ones, as well as calls for discrimination on national, racial, sexual or other grounds;

 

- violation of generally accepted norms of morality and morality;

 

5.1.it is not allowed to place ads of a marketing nature, that is, ads advertising not a specific product or service offered, but a set of goods and services as a whole.

5.2. it is not allowed to place ads about dating.

 

6. User Responsibility

 

6.1. Each user who places his ad in the Lost and Found application is solely responsible for it.

 

7. personal data and confidentiality.

 

7.1. The Lost and Found application does not bind any confidentiality obligations, except for the requirements of the personal data processing policy and Belarusian legislation.

 

Forbidden things

 

The list of things in respect of which it is prohibited to place ads in the LOST and FOUND application:

 

- narcotic drugs, psychotropic substances, their precursors;

 

- liquids for electronic smoking systems;

 

- military products, ammunition, means of active protection and all types of weapons;

 

- products structurally similar to weapons

 

- products intended for the suppression of mass riots and dispersal of processions, including appropriate vehicles and equipment, with the exception of products intended for fire fighting;

 

- means of fire protection, including mobile means according to the type of shields, as well as means providing stable mechanical protection, with the exception of means intended for protection during sports or for individual protection in peaceful work;

 

- toxic substances, as well as other similar chemicals that can harm human life or health;

 

- products that pose a potential danger to human health;

 

- human organs and (or) tissues, as well as offers related to human trafficking;

 

- products of a sexual, erotic and pornographic nature;

 

- means of contraception;

 

- medicines (including homeopathic), vitamins, herbs, plants and other natural remedies whose medicinal properties are widely known; biologically active additives (including weight loss products); sports nutrition

 

- medicines and vitamins for animals

 

- alcoholic beverages and tobacco products;

 

- explosive and pyrotechnic materials (for example, fireworks, fireworks, smoke bombs);

 

- contact lenses;

 

- infant formula;

 

- state awards of the Republic of Belarus and foreign countries;

 

- securities;

 

- foreign currency;

 

- cryptocurrency;

 

- personal documents, their forms, as well as forms of strict reporting;

 

- registration documents;

 

- mobile phone numbers;

 

- email addresses;

 

- online game accounts, game codes;

 

- objects that are exclusively owned by the state in accordance with the legislation of the Republic of Belarus;

 

- products in the name of which the term "electronic cigarette" is used;

 

- expired products;

 

- wild animals and wild plants belonging to the species included in the Red Book of the Republic of Belarus, as well as animal and plant species, their parts or derivatives thereof (derivatives) subject to the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora, signed in Washington on March 3, 1973 (CITES);

 

- skins of cats and dogs.
